RailTrack Performance Dashboard Layout (Wireframe)

Suggested Power BI layout for a clean and balanced dashboard

Distance Traveled Over Time

Line Chart
X: Date | Y: Sum of distance_km

Fuel Consumption Over Time

Line Chart
X: Date | Y: Sum of fuel_used_l

Maintenance Distribution (%)

Donut Chart
Category: maintenance_flag | Value: Count of railcar_id

Key Metrics (KPIs)

  • Total Railcars — Count of railcar_id
  • Avg Operating Temp (°C) — Average(temperature_c)
  • Avg Fuel Efficiency (km/L) — distance_km / fuel_used_l
  • Maintenance Rate (%) — (Sum(maintenance_flag)/Count(railcar_id))*100

Filters: Date | Railcar ID | Maintenance Flag

Optional: Add slicers and a 'Last Updated' field at the bottom right
